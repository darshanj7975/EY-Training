| Domain      | STM                                | LTM                                |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Commute     | Current transport funding requests | Historical infrastructure spending |
| Defence     | Current procurement requests       | Multi-year defence strategy        |
| Agriculture | Current crop support needs         | Past subsidy and yield trends      |
| Government  | Current department demands         | Historical operational expenditure |


Summary:

STM = current year's budgeting context and active planning information.
LTM = historical budgets, policies, trends, regulations, and domain knowledge accumulated over multiple years.
The Planner Agent combines both STM and LTM to generate a realistic annual budget allocation plan.
