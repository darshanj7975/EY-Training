Framework Chosen:

LangChain + LlamaIndex

LangChain → Email classification, workflow orchestration, API/tool calling, escalation logic.
LlamaIndex → Retrieval of policy documents and knowledge-base search.

Step 1:
Email Ingestion & Classification

Incoming customer email enters the system.
LangChain classifies it into categories:
Delivery Delay
Damaged Package
Refund Request
Tracking Issue
General Inquiry

Step 2:
Retrieve Relevant Policies

Based on the category, LlamaIndex searches the logistics policy knowledge base.
Retrieves relevant SOPs, refund rules, shipping policies, and escalation guidelines.

Step 3:
Generate Draft Reply (RAG)

LangChain sends:
Customer email
Retrieved policy documents
Company response template to LLM
LLM generates a draft response with citations from the policy documents.

Step 4:
Human Escalation via Ticketing API
LangChain agent evaluates:
Confidence score
Sentiment
Complexity
If complex:
Create ticket via ServiceNow/Zendesk/Jira API.
Route to human support agent.

If complex:
Create ticket via ServiceNow/Zendesk/Jira API.
Route to human support agent.
Otherwise:
Send draft response automatically.

Justification:

LangChain excels at multi-step workflows, tool calling, agents, and API integrations.
LlamaIndex is optimized for retrieval over large document repositories and knowledge bases.
The combination enables a scalable RAG pipeline for 10,000+ emails per day.
Human-in-the-loop escalation improves reliability for edge cases.
Retrieved policy documents reduce hallucinations and improve response accuracy.
